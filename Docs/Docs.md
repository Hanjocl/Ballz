### Kinetic Energy

The total kinetic energy is:

$$
E_{\text{kin}} = \frac{1}{2} M v^2 + \frac{1}{2} I \omega^2
$$

For a hollow sphere:

$$
E_{\text{kin}} = \frac{1}{2} M v^2 + \frac{1}{2} \left(\frac{2}{3} M R^2 \right) \omega^2
$$
